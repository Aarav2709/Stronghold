Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers to help you prepare for Singularity 2025.

Who can participate in Singularity?

Singularity is open to all high school students from any school. Teams must consist of 2-4 members from the same school.

Do I need prior programming experience?

While programming experience is helpful, it's not strictly required. We welcome participants of all skill levels and provide mentorship during the event.

What should teams bring to the competition?

Bring your laptops, chargers, any software/tools you prefer to use, and your enthusiasm! We'll provide power outlets, internet, and refreshments.

Is there a registration fee?

No, participation in Singularity is completely free! This includes access to mentors, materials, and refreshments throughout the day.

Can teams participate remotely?

Yes! We offer hybrid participation. Remote teams can join virtually, though in-person attendance is recommended for the full experience.

How are projects judged?

Projects are evaluated based on innovation, technical implementation, presentation quality, and potential impact. Our panel includes industry professionals and educators.
